Footage: stressed writer, laptop, social feeds, then confident author.

## 2) Social proof (reader love)
Voiceover: "The best marketing is other people. RevenuePress AI creates share cards and referral loops that readers actually use." 
CTA: "Get your share kit."
Footage: people reading, sharing on phone, notifications.

## 3) The gift angle (give first)
Voiceover: "Give your audience a gift: a free chapter, workbook, or audio snippet — and watch conversions jump." 
CTA: "Generate your gift funnel."
Footage: download animation, email signup, happy customer.

## 4) The identity angle (who you become)
Voiceover: "This book isn’t information. It’s a new identity. RevenuePress AI finds the communities that already want it." 
CTA: "Find your people."
Footage: niche communities, events, online groups.

## 5) The urgency angle (limited slots)
Voiceover: "Hot books get momentum. RevenuePress AI pushes your launch into a 14-day sprint and tracks what works."

Footage: countdown, analytics, book cover hero.
